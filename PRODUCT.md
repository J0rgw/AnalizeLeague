# Product

## Register
product

## Users
Professional League of Legends analysts and coaches at competitive teams. Context: dim room, late hours, multiple scrims back-to-back. Primary job: build a debrief from the day's replays before tomorrow's session.

## Product Purpose
AI assistant that replaces hours of manual replay review. Outputs a ranked review agenda with VOD timestamps and a scrim Q&A interface. Saves analyst time by automating the heaviest cognitive work: fight detection, gold diffs, objective trades, jungle pathing. Not a stats dashboard.

## Brand Personality
Clinical, precise, fast. The tool disappears into the workflow. Information earns its place; decoration doesn't.

## Anti-references
- op.gg, Porofessor: consumer stats dashboards, busy column layouts, color-overloaded
- Generic SaaS (HubSpot, Monday): rounded corners, pastel gradients, friendly-brand aesthetic
- Neon esports (Liquipedia, TFT): electric cyan/purple, glow effects, heavy brand saturation

## Design Principles
1. Information earns space. Every element serves an analyst decision, not a layout gap.
2. The tool disappears. Fast, predictable UI that stays out of the way.
3. Precision over flair. Hierarchy through scale and weight, not decoration.
4. State is always visible. Loading, error, empty states are never an afterthought.
5. Clinical warmth. Dark but not cold. Warm neutrals, one grounded accent.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA minimum. 4.5:1 contrast for body text, 3:1 for large text. Win/loss and side indicators always carry both color and text label.
